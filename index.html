How do I join a tournament?

To join a tournament, simply navigate to the 'Tournaments' section, select the event you want to participate in, and hit the 'Join' button. Get ready to showcase your skills and compete for amazing prizes!

Can I stream my gameplay live?

Absolutely! ESO provides you with the tools to stream your gameplay live for the world to see. Share your gaming prowess, engage with your audience, and build your gaming community like a pro.

How can I win rewards on ESO?

Winning rewards on ESO is all about skill, dedication, and strategy. Compete in tournaments, climb the leaderboard, engage with the community, and stay active on the platform to unlock exciting rewards and recognition.
